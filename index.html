Bangladesh 2.0

Idea, innovation, vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Foster an ecosystem that supports startups, encourages tech-driven solutions, and invests in research and development (R&D).

Sustainability

Ensure sustainable use of natural resources while combating climate change.

Infrastructure

Develop world-class transport systems, energy grids, and communication networks to support economic growth.

Empowerment

Empower individuals, especially marginalized groups, through education, skill development, and equitable opportunities.
